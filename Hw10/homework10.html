Homework 10: Geoprocessing Analysis

Isabel O'Brien

I wanted to make this map becuase my dad often likes to get coffee when we go on bike rides, and I wanted to display which bike trails were close to a Starbucks. To do this, I found data showing the locations of every Starbucks in the US, the greenway trails in Wake County, and an outline of Wake County itself. I then used the buffer tool to create buffers around each Starbucks location with a radius of 1,000 feet. I then used the intersection tool to highlight where these buffers overlapped with the trails. The result was a map that clearly shows which trails we can ride on and still easily get coffee!
